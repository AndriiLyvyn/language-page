Podróże transport

Rozmowa o podróży pociągiem

Traveler 1: Excuse me, do you know which platform the train to London leaves from?

Traveler 2: Yes, it’s platform 5. The train should be arriving in about 10 minutes.

Traveler 1: Thank you! Do I need to reserve a seat, or can I just buy a ticket?

Traveler 2: You can buy a ticket, but it’s a good idea to reserve a seat, especially during busy times.

Traveler 1: That makes sense. Do you know if there’s a dining car on the train?

Traveler 2: Yes, there is. They serve snacks, drinks, and light meals.

Traveler 1: Great! I’ll make sure to grab something during the trip.

Traveler 2: Have a safe journey!

Traveler 1: Thanks! You too.
