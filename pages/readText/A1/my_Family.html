My Family

Hello! My name is Sara. I am 8 years old. I live with my family in a small house in the city. My family is small but very happy. I have a mother, a father, and a brother. We also have a pet dog named Max.

My mother's name is Emma. She is 35 years old and she is a teacher. She teaches math at a primary school. Every day, she wakes up early to prepare breakfast for us. She is very kind and always helps me with my homework. She loves to read books and sometimes, she reads stories to me and my brother before we go to bed. Her favorite color is blue and she likes to wear blue dresses. On weekends, she likes to bake cookies and cakes. We help her in the kitchen, and it is always a lot of fun.

My father's name is John. He is 40 years old and he is a doctor. He works in a big hospital. He helps sick people get better. He is very smart and always knows what to do when someone is sick. He works long hours, but he always finds time to play with us when he comes home. He likes to play soccer and we often play together in the park. His favorite food is pizza and he makes the best pizza in the world. He also likes to watch movies and sometimes, we have movie nights at home.

My brother's name is Tom. He is 5 years old and he is very funny. He likes to play with his toys and watch cartoons. He goes to kindergarten. He likes to draw pictures and his favorite color is red. He has a lot of energy and he is always running around. Sometimes, he can be a little annoying, but I love him very much. We play together every day. He likes to play with cars and trucks. We also play hide and seek. He always hides in the same place, but I pretend not to find him right away because it makes him happy.

Our dog, Max, is a part of our family too. He is a Golden Retriever and he is very friendly. He loves to play fetch and he always brings the ball back to us. He likes to eat dog biscuits and he sleeps in a small bed in the living room. Max is very protective of us and barks when someone new comes to the door. He also likes to go for walks in the park. We take him for a walk every evening. He is very good with Tom and they play together a lot.

On weekends, we do many fun activities together. Sometimes we go to the beach and build sandcastles. Other times, we go to the zoo to see the animals. My favorite animal is the elephant because it is so big and gentle. We also like to go to the park and have picnics. My mom makes sandwiches and we bring a blanket to sit on. We play games and sometimes, we ride our bikes. I have a pink bike with a basket in front, and Tom has a small blue bike with training wheels.

In the evenings, we like to sit together and talk about our day. My mom and dad always listen to what I have to say. They give me good advice and help me when I have problems. After dinner, we sometimes watch TV together. My favorite show is about animals. We also play board games. My favorite game is Monopoly because it is very exciting. My dad always wins, but we all have fun playing.

During the holidays, we sometimes go on trips. Last summer, we went to the mountains. It was very beautiful there. We stayed in a small cabin and went hiking every day. We saw many birds and animals. My dad took a lot of pictures. In the winter, we sometimes go to visit our grandparents. They live in a small village. My grandmother makes the best cookies, and my grandfather tells us interesting stories about when he was young.

I love my family very much. They are always there for me and make me feel safe and happy. We share many good times together and I am very grateful for them. I am lucky to have such a wonderful family.
