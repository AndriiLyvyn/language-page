My Daily Routine

Hello! My name is Jack. I am 11 years old and I want to tell you about my daily routine. I have a very busy day, but I enjoy every part of it.

I wake up at 6:30 in the morning. My alarm clock rings and I get out of bed. First, I brush my teeth and wash my face. Then, I get dressed. I usually wear a t-shirt and jeans. After that, I go to the kitchen for breakfast. I like to eat cereal and drink orange juice. Sometimes, I have toast with butter and jam.

At 7:30, I leave the house to go to school. My school is not far from my house, so I walk there with my friend Tom. We talk and laugh on the way to school. Our school starts at 8 o'clock. We have many lessons in the morning. My favorite lesson is PE because I like to play sports. We play soccer, basketball, and sometimes we run races. It is a lot of fun and it keeps us healthy and active.

At 10:30, we have a break. I eat a snack and play with my friends in the playground. We have swings, slides, and a sandbox. We also play games like tag and hide and seek. The break is short, but we have a lot of fun.

After the break, we continue with more lessons. We learn new things every day. I like science because we do experiments and learn about the world around us. My least favorite subject is math because it is difficult for me. But my teacher, Mr. Smith, helps me and I am getting better.

At 12 o'clock, we have lunch. I bring my lunch from home. I usually have a sandwich, an apple, and a juice box. Sometimes, I buy a snack from the school canteen. We eat in the cafeteria with all the other students. After lunch, we have some time to play before going back to class.

In the afternoon, we have more lessons and activities. On Wednesdays, we have art class. I like art class because we get to draw and paint. My favorite thing to draw is animals. I also like to make crafts with paper and glue. On Fridays, we have music class. We sing songs and play instruments. My favorite instrument is the guitar.

School ends at 3 o'clock. I walk home with Tom. We talk about our day and sometimes, we stop at the park to play for a while. When I get home, I do my homework. I have a desk in my room where I study. My mom helps me with my homework if I need it.

After I finish my homework, I have some free time. I like to read books or play video games. Sometimes, I watch TV or play with my dog, Max. Max is a Golden Retriever and he is very friendly. He likes to play fetch and he always brings the ball back to me.

In the evening, my family and I have dinner together at 7 o'clock. We talk about our day and share stories. We eat different foods like pasta, rice, and vegetables. My favorite food is spaghetti with meatballs. After dinner, I help clean up and then I get ready for bed.

I go to bed at 9 o'clock. I like to read a book before sleeping. My favorite books are adventure stories. I fall asleep quickly because I am usually tired after a busy day.

On weekends, I do not have school, but I still do many fun and interesting things. Sometimes, we go on trips or visit friends and family. I also like to ride my bike or go to the park. My friends and I sometimes have playdates. We play games, watch movies, and have a lot of fun together.

I also have hobbies that I enjoy in my free time. I like to build models with my dad. We build cars, airplanes, and ships. It is a lot of fun and it takes a lot of patience. I also like to draw and paint. I have a sketchbook where I draw pictures of animals, people, and places. I also like to play soccer with my friends. We have a small field near our house where we play. It is a lot of fun and it keeps us active and healthy.

My daily routine is very busy, but I enjoy every part of it. I like going to school, learning new things, and spending time with my family and friends. I am very happy with my life and I look forward to each new day.
