My School

Hello! My name is Mia. I am 9 years old and I go to Greenfield School. It is a big school with many students and teachers. I am in class 3A. My teacher's name is Mrs. Brown. She is very nice and helps us learn many things.

Every day, I wake up at 7 o'clock in the morning. I get ready for school and have breakfast. I usually eat toast with jam and drink a glass of milk. At 7:30, I leave the house. My school is not far from my house, so I walk there with my friend Emma. We talk and laugh on the way to school.

Our school starts at 8 o'clock. We have a big building with many classrooms. Each classroom has desks, chairs, a blackboard, and a computer. We also have a library, a gym, and a playground. Our school is very clean and well-organized.

In the morning, we have lessons in different subjects like math, science, English, and history. My favorite subject is art because I like to draw and paint. Mrs. Brown is our art teacher. She is very creative and always has new ideas for us. She encourages us to express ourselves through our art.

At 10 o'clock, we have a break. I eat a snack and play with my friends in the playground. We have swings, slides, and a sandbox. We also play games like tag and hide and seek. The break is short, but we have a lot of fun.

After the break, we continue with more lessons. We learn new things every day. I like science because we do experiments and learn about the world around us. My least favorite subject is math because it is difficult for me. But Mrs. Brown helps me and I am getting better.

At 12 o'clock, we have lunch. I bring my lunch from home. I usually have a sandwich, an apple, and a juice box. Sometimes, I buy a snack from the school canteen. We eat in the cafeteria with all the other students. After lunch, we have some time to play before going back to class.

In the afternoon, we have more lessons and activities. On Wednesdays, we have music class. I like music class because we get to sing and play instruments. My favorite instrument is the piano. We also have PE class twice a week. We play sports and do exercises. It is important to stay healthy and active.

School ends at 3 o'clock. I walk home with Emma. We talk about our day and sometimes, we stop at the park to play for a while. When I get home, I do my homework. I have a desk in my room where I study. My mom helps me with my homework if I need it.

After I finish my homework, I have some free time. I like to read books or play games. Sometimes, I watch TV or play with my dog. In the evening, my family and I have dinner together. We talk about our day and share stories. After dinner, I help clean up and then I get ready for bed.

On weekends, I do not have school, but I still do many fun and interesting things. Sometimes, we go on trips or visit friends and family. I also like to ride my bike or go to the park. My friends and I sometimes have playdates. We play games, watch movies, and have a lot of fun together.

I like my school because I learn new things and have fun with my friends. The teachers are nice and they help us when we need it. I am happy to be a student at Greenfield School. It is a great place to learn and grow. I look forward to going to school every day and seeing what new things I will learn.

My school also has special events and activities. We have a sports day where we compete in different games and races. We also have a talent show where students can perform and show their skills. Last year, I sang a song in the talent show and everyone clapped for me. It was a very special moment.

We also go on field trips to interesting places. Last month, we went to the science museum. We saw many cool exhibits and learned about space and the planets. It was a very exciting trip and I had a lot of fun with my friends.

Our school also has a library with many books. I like to go there and read. I borrow books and take them home to read. My favorite books are about animals and adventures. The librarian, Mrs. Green, is very nice. She helps us find books and sometimes reads stories to us.

Greenfield School is a great place to be. I am learning a lot and making many friends. I am very happy to be a student there. I hope to continue learning and having fun every day. My school is a very important part of my life and I love it very much.
